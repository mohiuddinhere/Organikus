ABOUT US

ORGANICUS is a multi-faceted communication and community engagement initiative in Bangladesh that aims to increase access to modern agricultural advances for long-term food security. It is based on Chattogram in general.

ORGANICUS disseminates evidence-based information about the social, economic, and environmental benefits of modern agricultural innovations, such as the use of biotechnology to breed crops, particularly those that can improve the nutritional content of staple foods like vitamin- and mineral-enriched rice. ORGANICUS was founded on the belief that agricultural innovations can help feed Bangladesh's 160 million people while also providing farmers and customers with safer, better, and more nutritious food.

We provide a hardiness zone to assist producers in determining whether crops would thrive in a specific area or city. The fundamental idea behind this is that it will compute the annual temperature and then advise growers about it, allowing them to increase crop yield.
